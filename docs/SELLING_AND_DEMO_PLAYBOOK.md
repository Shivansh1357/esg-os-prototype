# ESG Console Selling and Demo Playbook

This guide explains what each module does, how to demonstrate it, and how to position value in a sales conversation.

## 1) One-line Value Proposition
ESG Console reduces time-to-report for ESG disclosures by turning fragmented data collection into an auditable, evidence-backed workflow from intake to frozen reporting.

## 2) Buyer Personas
- **Economic buyer:** CFO / Finance Transformation
- **Functional buyer:** Head of ESG / Sustainability Lead
- **Technical buyer:** CIO / Data Platform Lead
- **Assurance buyer:** Internal Audit / External Auditor teams

## 3) Core Modules and Their Business Value

## Onboarding
What it does:
- Configures reporting defaults (framework, FY, currency, units).

Value:
- Standardizes reporting setup quickly across teams.

## Users + Entities
What it does:
- Sets up users and organizational entities (ORG/BU/SITE).

Value:
- Creates the ownership model needed for distributed data collection.

## Data Hub
What it does:
- Uploads files, maps columns, creates draft facts, and approves trusted records.

Value:
- Converts raw operational data into governed ESG facts.

## Emissions Explorer
What it does:
- Shows Scope 1/2/3 totals with period comparison and recalculation controls.

Value:
- Enables fast analysis and defensible emissions updates.

## Compliance Gap Map
What it does:
- Tracks PASS/FAIL/RISK rules and attaches evidence to resolve gaps.

Value:
- Makes compliance remediation measurable and auditable.

## Reports
What it does:
- Generates drafts, exports reports, and freezes versions for audit use.

Value:
- Produces reproducible outputs and eliminates reporting ambiguity.

## Suppliers (Scope 3)
What it does:
- Sends supplier invites, collects submissions, approves responses, and tracks coverage.

Value:
- Expands Scope 3 coverage with supplier-grade evidence trails.

## Executive Cockpit
What it does:
- Surfaces KPI summaries, trend deltas, and snapshot/live mode context.

Value:
- Gives leadership decision-ready ESG signals.

## Audit
What it does:
- Displays event logs across facts/compliance/freeze/supplier actions.

Value:
- Provides traceability for assurance and governance teams.

## Pilot Dashboard
What it does:
- Tracks activation and usage metrics for tenant rollouts.

Value:
- Measures adoption and implementation ROI.

## Supplier Public Form
What it does:
- Tokenized external submission page with bilingual support and evidence upload.

Value:
- Low-friction supplier participation with controlled access.

## 4) 12–15 Minute Demo Flow
1. Start at `/onboarding` to frame setup simplicity.
2. Show `/admin/entities` to demonstrate organizational modeling.
3. Move to `/data`, run upload + mapping + approval.
4. Open `/emissions` and explain quarter-over-quarter impact.
5. Open `/compliance`, resolve one finding with evidence.
6. Open `/reports`, generate export and freeze.
7. Open `/exec` and show live vs snapshot decision context.
8. Open `/suppliers` and show coverage metrics.
9. End at `/audit` to prove traceability.

## 5) Key Talking Points by Outcome
- **Speed:** “From source file to board-ready report in one controlled workflow.”
- **Control:** “Every metric has source evidence and approval status.”
- **Auditability:** “Frozen snapshots + event logs remove disputes during assurance.”
- **Scalability:** “Supplier and entity workflows scale across business units.”

## 6) Common Objections and Responses
- “How do we trust the numbers?”
  - Use approval states, evidence links, freeze snapshots, and audit logs.
- “Can this handle evolving frameworks?”
  - Config-driven setup and modular workflow allow framework-specific extensions.
- “How do auditors work with this?”
  - Frozen mode, lineage views, and export flows are built for assurance.

## 7) Commercial Positioning (Prototype to MVP)
- Pilot with one tenant and one reporting cycle.
- Success criteria:
  - Time-to-first-report
  - Coverage uplift (especially Scope 3)
  - Number of unresolved compliance gaps
  - Export and assurance readiness
