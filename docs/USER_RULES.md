# User Rules & Acceptable Use (v1.0.0)

> Applies to all end-users of the ESG OS platform (tenants, contributors, suppliers, auditors).

## Roles & Access
- Access is **role-based**: Admin, Sustainability, Compliance, Contributor, Procurement, Executive Viewer, Auditor.
- Accounts are **individual**; sharing credentials is prohibited.
- Auditors receive **time-boxed** read-only links.

## Data Responsibility
- Upload only relevant ESG data (utility bills, HR counts, etc.).
- Do not upload personal IDs, financial account numbers, or unrelated PII.
- Ensure data accuracy; falsification may result in account suspension.

## Evidence & Reports
- Evidence files must be legible; re-uploads are allowed.
- Reports marked **LOCKED** are read-only; changes require a new version.

## Supplier Portal
- Suppliers must provide truthful responses and upload supporting evidence.
- Proxy/estimated values must be clearly labeled; primary data preferred.

## AI Features
- AI-generated text is a **draft** and must be reviewed before publishing.
- AI suggestions do not constitute compliance advice.

## Security & Privacy
- Do not attempt to access other tenants’ data.
- Report suspected breaches to the support channel immediately.
- Downloads and exports are for internal use unless otherwise contracted.

## Support & SLAs
- Core platform availability target **99.9%** monthly.
- Response times: Critical (4h), High (1 business day), Normal (2 business days).

## Termination
- We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts for violations of these rules.
