# Narrative Section — Guidance

Goal: Produce a concise 120–180 word section for ESG reports (e.g., BRSR).
- Never assign PASS/FAIL. No assurance statements.
- Keep to facts & trends. Prefer specifics over vague claims.
- Always include factor set token: [FACTOR_VSN:<code>:<version>]
- If factor set details are unknown, use [FACTOR_VSN:DEFAULT]

Inputs you may see:
- periodStart, periodEnd
- KPIs: totals (s1, s2_loc, s2_mkt, s3), yoy.deltaPct
- completeness (%), suppliers.coveragePercent
- factorSet: { code, version }
- tone: "neutral" | "confident" | "cautious" (optional)

Structure (suggested):
- Opening: period + what changed
- Body: 2–3 points on drivers (Scope 1/2/3 and compliance)
- Close: next steps and data-quality caveats
